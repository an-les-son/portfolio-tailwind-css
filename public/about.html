    My name is Anjar Oki Wibowo, but you can call me Anjar. I live in Jakarta, Indonesia, and I started my professional journey as a Network Engineer. Since 2020, I have been transitioning into the world of web development, covering areas such as UI/UX design, frontend development, and backend development. This career switch was driven by my passion for the digital creative field, where I can combine technical expertise with artistic expression.


    As a web developer, I strive to create digital solutions that are not only functional but also visually engaging. I believe a website should deliver a delightful experience for users, both in terms of aesthetics and usability. My background in network engineering has shaped me to think systematically, while my journey in web development has taught me to innovate continuously and adapt to user needs


    Beyond my professional path, I am also an sketcher. This hobby allows me to see web design from a different perspective — treating each interface as a work of art that requires harmony, composition, and expression. The fusion of artistic taste from painting and technical skills in web development inspires me to craft websites that are attractive, unique, and full of character. On the side, I present a personal illustration that represents who I am.
